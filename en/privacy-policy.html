Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 31, 2026

This privacy policy aims to inform you how the "SpringloBook", "SpringloMagic", and "SpringloView" applications collect, use, share, and protect user information.

About the Applications

Developed to enrich the augmented reality experience, these free applications use Augmented Reality (AR) to bring our products to life. By pointing the device at the product, the user can interact with animations and virtual content. The applications are ad-free and do not offer in-app purchases.

Information Collection

These applications do not collect, store, or share personal user information. No identifiable information is captured or transmitted during the use of the applications.

Use of Permissions

The applications request access to the device's camera exclusively to enable the Augmented Reality functionality. The camera is used in real-time to detect and track physical markers or objects, allowing for the overlay of digital content. No images or videos are stored, persisted, or shared externally. All processing is performed locally on the user's device.

Security

While these applications do not collect data, we use industry best practices to ensure that data processed locally on your device is protected against unauthorized access.

External Links

This application may direct users to our official website www.springlo-tech.com.br, which may contain partner links (such as Uiclap) where our products can be purchased. We have no control over the privacy practices of these partners. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies on partner websites before providing any personal information.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy. Changes will be communicated through an update within the application. We recommend that you review this policy regularly.
